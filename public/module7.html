Prospecting · Lesson 1

Apollo.io — Find 100 Verified Prospects in 10 Minutes

Apollo.io is a B2B database with over 275 million contacts. You can find verified email addresses, phone numbers and LinkedIn profiles of any business owner in any industry in any country — in minutes, for free.

What is Apollo.io and Why We Use It
Apollo.io is the most powerful free prospecting tool available. The free plan gives you 50 verified email exports per month — enough to start. You search by job title, industry, company size, location, and even the technology a company uses. Every result includes a verified business email address.
This is not just a list of names. Apollo shows you the CEO's direct email, the company's LinkedIn, their estimated revenue, how many employees they have, and what software they use. This data makes your outreach hyper-targeted and relevant.
Step 1 — Create Your Free Apollo.io Account
1
Go to apollo.io and click "Get started for free"
Open your browser and go to apollo.io. On the homepage click the button that says Get started for free. A registration page appears.
2
Sign up with Google for the fastest setup
Click Sign up with Google. Select your Google account. Apollo creates your account automatically and takes you to the dashboard. No credit card needed for the free plan.
Use a business email if possible — not a Gmail with a random name. Apollo's free plan gives you 50 email exports per month and 5 phone number exports. For your first client campaigns this is more than enough.
3
Connect your LinkedIn account (recommended)
Apollo will ask you to connect LinkedIn. Click Connect LinkedIn and log in. This gives Apollo access to LinkedIn data which dramatically improves the quality and accuracy of the contact information you find.
If you prefer not to connect LinkedIn right now, click "Skip" — you can still use Apollo without it. The contact data will be slightly less detailed but still very useful.
Step 2 — Find Your First 100 Prospects
1
Click "Search" in the top navigation
In the Apollo dashboard, look at the top navigation bar. Click on Search. A dropdown appears with two options: "People" and "Companies." Click People — this searches for individual contacts, not company profiles.
2
Set the Job Title filter
On the People search page, look at the left sidebar — this is where all the filters are. Find the Job Title filter. Click on it. A text field appears. Type the job titles you want to target, pressing Enter after each one:
For restaurant/food industry: Owner, Manager, Restaurant Owner

For real estate: Real Estate Agent, Broker, Agency Owner

For e-commerce: CEO, Founder, E-commerce Manager

For general B2B: CEO, Founder, Managing Director, Operations Manager
3
Set the Industry filter
Find the Industry filter in the left sidebar. Click on it. Type your target industry and select from the dropdown suggestions. Examples: Restaurants, Real Estate, Health, Wellness and Fitness, Retail.
4
Set the Location filter
Find the Location filter. Click it and type your target city or country. Examples: Bucharest, Romania or Romania or United Kingdom. Select the suggestion that appears.
5
Set the Company Size filter (optional but powerful)
Find the Company Headcount or # Employees filter. Select 1–10 and 11–50. This filters for small businesses — the ones most likely to need your automation services and most likely to have the owner as the decision maker.
Avoid targeting companies with 200+ employees for your first clients. Large companies have procurement processes, multiple decision makers, and long sales cycles. Small businesses (1–50 employees) can say yes on a call and pay the same day.
6
Review your results and check the numbers
After setting your filters, Apollo shows you the total number of matching contacts on the right side. You want between 200 and 2,000 results — enough to run multiple campaigns. If you have fewer than 50 results, broaden your filters (larger location, more job titles). If you have more than 10,000, add more specific filters.
7
Select 100 contacts and export to CSV
At the top of the results list, check the box to select all contacts on the current page (usually 25 per page). Select 4 pages to get 100 contacts total. Then click the Export button — it looks like a download arrow icon. Select Export to CSV.
Apollo will use your free export credits. The free plan gives 50 email exports/month. If you need more, either:
— Create a second free account with a different email
— Upgrade to the Basic plan ($49/month) which gives 300 exports
— Use the exported contacts multiple times across different campaigns

The CSV file downloads to your computer. Open it to verify the data — you should see columns for First Name, Last Name, Email, Company, Title, LinkedIn URL, and more.
✦ The Technology Filter — Find The Best Prospects
Apollo has a "Technologies Used" filter that shows what software a company uses. Search for companies using HubSpot or Salesforce — these companies already have a CRM, which means they care about their sales process and are much more receptive to AI automation. They already understand the concept of tools that improve efficiency. Filter by technology to dramatically increase your reply rates.
Key Takeaways — Lesson 1
Apollo.io free plan gives 50 email exports/month — enough for your first campaigns. No credit card needed.
Always filter by Company Size 1–50 employees for your first clients — they decide faster and pay faster.
The Technology filter (HubSpot/Salesforce users) gives you pre-qualified prospects who already value automation tools.
Export 100 contacts at a time to a CSV — this becomes the input for your Instantly.ai campaign in Lesson 2.
✦ Action — Export Your First 100 Prospects Now
Go to apollo.io right now and create your free account. Set up the filters for your chosen industry and location. Export 100 contacts to a CSV file. Open the CSV and verify you have real names and email addresses. Save the file as "Prospects — [Industry] — [Date].csv" — you will upload it to Instantly.ai in Lesson 2.
Email Sending · Lesson 2

Instantly.ai — Send 50 Personalized Emails Per Day Automatically

Instantly.ai is the platform that sends cold emails at scale without landing in spam. It warms up your email address, manages sending limits, handles follow-ups, and tracks replies — all automatically.

Why Instantly.ai and Not Just Gmail
If you send 50 cold emails from a regular Gmail account, Google will flag your account as spam within days and suspend it. Instantly.ai solves this by: warming up your email address gradually (so Gmail and email providers trust it), spacing out sends to look natural, rotating between multiple email addresses if needed, and tracking opens, clicks, and replies.
The result: your emails land in the inbox — not the spam folder — and you can send consistently without getting your account suspended.
Step 1 — Create Your Instantly.ai Account
1
Go to instantly.ai and click "Start Free Trial"
Open instantly.ai in your browser. Click the Start Free Trial or Get Started button. Fill in your name, email, and create a password. Click Create Account.
Instantly.ai has a 14-day free trial on their Growth plan ($37/month after trial). The Growth plan lets you send up to 5,000 emails/month across unlimited email accounts. Start the trial — you can cancel before it charges if you decide not to continue.
2
Connect your sending email address
After creating your account, Instantly.ai prompts you to connect an email account. Click Connect Email Account. You have two options:
Option A — Google Workspace (recommended): If you have a custom domain email like yourname@youragency.com, connect it here. Click Google → authorize access. Custom domain emails have much better deliverability than Gmail addresses.

Option B — Regular Gmail: Click Google and connect your Gmail. This works but has lower deliverability. Gmail accounts are more likely to get flagged as spam for cold outreach.

For best results: buy a cheap domain ($10/year at Namecheap) and create a Google Workspace email ($6/month). The improved deliverability is worth far more than this cost.
3
Turn on Email Warmup — do this BEFORE sending
This is the most important step. After connecting your email, find the Warmup section — it is in the left sidebar or in your email account settings. Toggle it ON.
Email warmup means Instantly.ai automatically sends and replies to emails from your account in the background — between real email addresses in their warmup network. This makes your email address look like it belongs to an active, trusted person.

Wait 14 days after turning on warmup before sending your first cold email campaign. This is non-negotiable. Starting a campaign before warmup is complete will get your emails sent to spam.

During warmup, Instantly sends 2–5 warmup emails per day from your account. You will see them in your Sent folder — ignore them, they are automated.
Step 2 — Create Your Cold Email Campaign
1
Click "Campaigns" in the left sidebar then "New Campaign"
In Instantly.ai, click Campaigns in the left navigation. Click the + New Campaign button. A campaign creation wizard opens.
2
Name your campaign
Give the campaign a clear name like "Restaurant Owners — Romania — October 2024." This helps you track performance across multiple campaigns. Click Continue.
3
Upload your Apollo CSV — add your prospects
The next step asks you to add leads. Click Upload CSV. Select the CSV file you exported from Apollo in Lesson 1. Instantly.ai shows a preview of your data — columns for first name, last name, email, company, etc.
Map the columns correctly — Instantly.ai asks you to match each column in your CSV to a variable name:

— CSV column "First Name" → map to {{firstName}}
— CSV column "Last Name" → map to {{lastName}}
— CSV column "Company" → map to {{companyName}}
— CSV column "Title" → map to {{jobTitle}}

These variable names are what you use in your email template to personalize each email automatically. Click Continue after mapping.
4
Write your email sequence — Email 1 (Initial Outreach)
The next step is writing your email. Click on the email template area. Fill in:
Subject line:
Quick question about {{companyName}}
Email body — paste this and customize:
Cold Email — Initial Outreach
Hi {{firstName}}, I came across {{companyName}} and noticed you likely receive a lot of customer inquiries — WhatsApp messages, contact form submissions, emails — that take significant time to respond to. I build AI automation systems that reply to every customer inquiry within seconds, 24/7, in your brand voice. Businesses using this stop losing leads to slow response times. Would it be worth a 15-minute call this week to show you exactly how it works for {{companyName}}? [Your Name]
Keep it under 100 words. Short emails get more replies than long ones. The {{firstName}} and {{companyName}} variables are automatically replaced with real data from your CSV.
5
Add a Follow-Up Email (Email 2) — sent 3 days later
In Instantly.ai, after writing Email 1, click + Add Step to add a follow-up. Set the delay to 3 days. Write a short follow-up:
Follow-Up Email — Day 3
Hi {{firstName}}, Just following up on my note from a few days ago. I know timing might not be right — but if {{companyName}} is losing leads because of slow response times, I can show you a working solution in 15 minutes. Worth a quick look? [Your Name]
Maximum 2 follow-ups. After 2 emails with no reply, stop. Never send more — it damages your sender reputation.
6
Configure sending settings
Before launching, set these critical sending settings. Find the Settings or Schedule tab in the campaign:
Daily sending limit: Set to 30–50 emails per day. Never start with 50/day — start with 20/day for the first week, then increase to 50/day in week 2.

Sending hours: Set to Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM in the recipient's timezone. Emails sent during business hours get more opens.

Stop on reply: Make sure this is turned ON. This stops sending follow-ups to anyone who replies — prevents you from looking like a bot by sending follow-ups to people who already responded.

Tracking: Turn on open tracking and click tracking so you can see which emails are being read.
7
Launch the campaign
After configuring everything, click Launch Campaign. Instantly.ai starts sending emails according to your schedule. You can see real-time statistics in the campaign dashboard: emails sent, opens, clicks, replies.
Check the campaign stats every morning. When someone replies, their email thread appears in the Instantly.ai inbox — you can reply directly from there. The goal of the cold email is not to close on email — it is to get them to say "yes" to a 15-minute call. Reply to every positive response within 1 hour and suggest two specific times for a call.
⚠️ Do Not Skip Email Warmup
Sending cold emails without warming up your email address first will get your emails sent to spam and your account suspended within days. Wait 14 days after turning on warmup before launching any campaign. This is the single most common mistake beginners make. The 14-day wait feels frustrating but it is essential for long-term success.
Key Takeaways — Lesson 2
Turn on Email Warmup immediately after connecting your email. Wait 14 days before your first campaign.
Start with 20 emails/day for week 1, then increase to 50/day. Never start at maximum volume.
Always turn on "Stop on reply" — this prevents follow-ups going to people who already responded.
The goal of cold email is to get a "yes" to a call — not to close a deal via email.
Full System · Lesson 3

Connect Apollo + Instantly + Make.com Into One Flow

The final step: connect Apollo, Instantly.ai and Make.com so the entire outreach process runs automatically. New prospects are imported, emails are personalized with GPT-4o, and replies trigger immediate follow-up actions — all without manual work.

The Complete Automated Outreach Flow
Here is the full flow you are building:
1
Apollo → Google Sheets (manual import, automated processing)
You export your Apollo CSV and upload it to a Google Sheet. Make.com watches this sheet for new rows and automatically processes each new prospect.
2
Google Sheets → OpenAI (personalized first line)
For each new prospect, Make.com sends their data to GPT-4o to generate a personalized opening line for the email. This first line is unique for every prospect and dramatically increases reply rates.
3
OpenAI → Instantly.ai API (add lead with personalized email)
Make.com sends the prospect's data plus the GPT-generated first line to Instantly.ai via their API, adding the lead to your campaign automatically.
4
Instantly.ai → Gmail (sends the email automatically)
Instantly.ai sends the personalized email from your connected sending address at the right time, with warmup running in the background to protect deliverability.
Step 1 — Set Up the Google Sheet as Input
1
Create a new Google Sheet called "Outreach Prospects"
Open sheets.google.com. Create a new spreadsheet. Name it "Outreach Prospects — [Industry]." Set up these column headers in Row 1:
Column A: First Name
Column B: Last Name
Column C: Email
Column D: Company
Column E: Job Title
Column F: Industry
Column G: Processed (leave empty — Make.com writes "YES" here after processing each row)
2
Copy your Apollo CSV data into the sheet
Open your Apollo CSV export in Excel or Google Sheets. Copy the relevant columns and paste them into your Outreach Prospects sheet starting at Row 2. Make sure each column matches the headers you created.
Step 2 — Build the Make.com Scenario
1
Create a new Scenario with Google Sheets trigger
In Make.com, create a new Scenario. Click the trigger circle. Search Google Sheets and select Watch New Rows. Connect your Google account. Select the "Outreach Prospects" spreadsheet and the Sheet tab. Set Limit to 5 — this processes 5 new prospects per run. Click OK.
Why Limit = 5? If you set Limit = 100, Make.com tries to process all 100 rows at once which costs 100 operations. Setting it to 5 means each scheduled run processes 5 rows, spreading the cost over multiple runs. Set the scenario to run every 15 minutes.
2
Add OpenAI to generate a personalized first line
Click + after Google Sheets. Add OpenAI → Create a Chat Completion. Connect your API key. Select gpt-4o. In the Message content field, use this prompt with variables from the sheet:
Write a single personalized opening sentence for a cold email to {{1.First_Name}} who is a {{1.Job_Title}} at {{1.Company}} in the {{1.Industry}} industry. The sentence should reference something specific about their role or industry that makes it feel personal and relevant. It should transition naturally into an email about AI automation services. Write ONLY the opening sentence — nothing else. Maximum 25 words. Sound natural, not salesy.
Example output from GPT-4o: "Managing customer inquiries for a growing restaurant group must take an enormous amount of your team's time every day." — This is unique for every prospect and makes the email feel hand-written.
3
Add HTTP module to call Instantly.ai API
Click + after OpenAI. Add HTTP → Make a Request. Configure it to add the prospect to your Instantly.ai campaign:
URL:
https://api.instantly.ai/api/v1/lead/add
Method: POST

Headers:
— Content-Type: application/json

Body (Raw JSON):
{ "api_key": "YOUR_INSTANTLY_API_KEY", "campaign_id": "YOUR_CAMPAIGN_ID", "skip_if_in_workspace": true, "leads": [ { "email": "{{1.Email}}", "first_name": "{{1.First_Name}}", "last_name": "{{1.Last_Name}}", "company_name": "{{1.Company}}", "personalization": "{{2.choices[].message.content}}" } ] }
Where to find your Instantly API Key: In Instantly.ai → click your profile icon top right → API Keys → Create API Key → copy it.

Where to find your Campaign ID: In Instantly.ai → Campaigns → click your campaign → look at the URL — the campaign ID is the long string after "/campaigns/"
4
Update the "Processed" column in Google Sheets
Add one more module after the HTTP call. Click +. Search Google Sheets and select Update a Row. Connect to your Outreach Prospects sheet. Set the Row Number to the current row variable from Module 1. In column G (Processed), type YES.
This marks each processed row so Make.com does not process the same prospect twice. Every time the scenario runs, it only picks up rows where column G is empty — skipping rows already marked "YES".
5
Update your Instantly.ai email template to use the personalization variable
Go back to Instantly.ai. Open your campaign. Edit the email template. In the email body, add the personalization variable at the very beginning:
{{personalization}} I noticed {{companyName}} likely handles a lot of customer inquiries daily that take significant time to respond to. I build AI automation systems that reply to every customer inquiry within seconds, 24/7, in your brand voice. Would a 15-minute call this week make sense to show you how it works? [Your Name]
The {{personalization}} variable will be replaced with the unique GPT-generated opening sentence for each prospect. The rest of the email is the same for everyone — but that first sentence makes every email feel personal and handwritten.
6
Turn on the Make.com Scenario and test
In Make.com, turn on the Scheduling toggle. Set it to run every 15 minutes. Click Run once to test. Check your Instantly.ai campaign — you should see new leads appearing in the campaign dashboard within seconds.
After the test run, go to your Google Sheet — the rows that were processed should now show "YES" in column G. This confirms the full flow is working.
The Expected Results — Realistic Numbers
Here is what to expect from a well-run cold email campaign using this system:
📊
Volume and Response Rates
Sending 50 emails per day × 20 working days = 1,000 emails per month. With good personalization and targeting, expect:
Open rate: 40–60% (industry average is 20% — personalized emails open more)
Reply rate: 3–8% (industry average is 1–2% — personalized first lines double reply rates)
1,000 emails × 5% reply rate = 50 conversations
50 conversations × 20% close rate = 10 calls booked
10 calls × 30% close rate = 3 new clients per month

3 new clients per month at $200/month average retainer = $600 new recurring revenue added every month. After 6 months that is $3,600/month from cold email clients alone.
✓ This System Runs Itself
Once everything is set up, your only job is to add new prospects to the Google Sheet periodically. Make.com processes them automatically, GPT-4o personalizes each email, and Instantly.ai sends them on schedule. You wake up every morning to replies from interested prospects already in your inbox. The system works while you sleep.
Key Takeaways — Lesson 3
The "Processed" column in Google Sheets prevents duplicate processing — always include it.
GPT-4o generated first lines increase reply rates significantly compared to generic templates.
Realistic expectation: 3–8% reply rate from 1,000 emails = 30–80 conversations per month.
The full system runs automatically once built — add new prospects to the Sheet and everything else happens without you.
